The Ising Model

The Ising model gives a clean mathematical framework for how local interactions can lead to collective alignment. In one dimension, the conventional result says no spontaneous magnetisation occurs â€” but with interaction ranges above a critical threshold, self-organisation is possible.

Equation (rendered with LaTeX):

$$ H = -J \sum_{i=1}^N \sum_{r=1}^R s_i s_{i+r} - h \sum_{i=1}^N s_i $$

where \( s_i \in \{-1,+1\} \) are spins, \( J \) is the coupling constant, \( h \) an external field, and \( R \) the interaction range.
